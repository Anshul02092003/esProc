Description:

  Create association between multiple sequences through the join key.

Syntax:

Note:

The function associates sequence A and sequence Ai through the join key and returns a sequence consisting of x:F,¡­ and xi:Fi,¡­; by default, x:F,¡­ involves all fields of A.

When relationship between A and Ai is one-to-many, xi: is an aggregate expression.

When relationship between A and Ai is many-to-one, records of Ai will appear repeatedly in the result set.

Parameter z specifies the join type. It can be absent or null. Perform an inner join when z is absent, and a left join when z is null; and only retain records of A that cannot find matches when z is null and both parameters xi:Fi are absent.
